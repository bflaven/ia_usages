Subject: Assistance with Chrome Browser Login/Logout and Private Session

Dear [User],

Thank you for reaching out to us for assistance with your Chrome browser. I understand that you are having trouble logging in and out and starting a private session. I am here to help.

To log out of your Chrome browser, follow these steps:

Click on the three dots in the upper-right corner of the Chrome window
Select "Sign out" from the dropdown menu
You will be logged out of your Google account and any other accounts you have signed into in Chrome.
To start a private session in Chrome, follow these steps:

Click on the three dots in the upper-right corner of the Chrome window
Select "New incognito window" from the dropdown menu
A new incognito window will open, and you can browse the web privately.
To log back in, follow these steps:

Click on the three dots in the upper-right corner of the Chrome window
Select "Sign in" from the dropdown menu
Enter your email address and password to sign in to your Google account.
If you have any further issues, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. We are here to help.
