COMET's liabilities and guarantees for privacy revolve primarily around the following key points:

### Privacy Guarantees and User Rights
- COMET claims to provide users with control over their personal data, including options to manage data collection, delete stored data like browsing data and passwords, and manage cookie preferences.
- Users can exercise rights such as accessing, deleting, or correcting their personal data held by COMET.
- COMET states it will not discriminate against users exercising these privacy rights and may verify identities before fulfilling requests.
- There is an option to designate authorized agents to act on a user's behalf with proper verification.
- COMET provides a “Do Not Track” request feature, though its effectiveness depends on the websites visited.[1][2]

### Limitations of Liability
- COMET disclaims liability for loss of data, damages, or any direct or indirect damages arising from the use or inability to use COMET services.
- This limitation covers situations like service availability, interactions with third parties via COMET, and use of software or systems powering COMET.
- Some jurisdictions may not allow such disclaimers fully, in which case this limitation might not apply.[3]

### Data Security Measures
- COMET servers are housed in secure data centers with physical and electronic access controls.
- Redundancies and backups are in place to ensure data availability and disaster recovery.
- Staff access to data is limited and controlled with multi-factor security methods.[4]

### Privacy Concerns and Risks
- Despite claims of privacy modes and local data storage, COMET's integration with cloud AI processing introduces risks of data leakage or unauthorized access.
- Security audits have revealed critical vulnerabilities, such as prompt injection attacks that could expose sensitive data.
- There is an inherent privacy paradox in COMET’s business model, which involves extensive data collection for advertising purposes, conflicting with users' privacy expectations.
- Critics note that while COMET offers privacy features, it still collects and processes data in ways that could compromise confidentiality.[5][6][7][8]

### Summary
COMET offers privacy guarantees to users, including rights over their personal data and implemented security measures. However, its liability disclaimers limit legal responsibility for damages related to data or service issues. Despite security precautions, there are documented risks and tensions between COMET’s data collection business model and privacy protections, emphasizing user caution.
