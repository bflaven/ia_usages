# Video Companions to Recent Posts

A small set of videos linked to my latest post. They serve primarily as personal references — a way to document processes I might otherwise forget — but they may be useful to others working on similar topics.

## Why Videos?

Practice beats theory. Forcing yourself to present technical work in English, however imperfectly, builds clarity of thought as much as language skill. These videos are low-budget and unpolished, and that's deliberate: if you can't communicate what you're doing, the work loses most of its value.

The editing is rough. The delivery is sometimes awkward. But there's a human behind it, and that still counts for something — especially at a time when most articles, presentations, and code are increasingly AI-generated. Video and audio remain, for now, more resistant to that drift.

## Context: Why These Topics Matter

All three videos sit at the intersection of a broader shift I'm tracking: the move from a **link economy** to a **response economy**, where traffic migrates from search engines to chatbots, and where SEO/SMO gives way to **GEO** (Generative Engine Optimization). Semantic clustering becomes structurally important in that context.

**Multilingualism** is the other thread running through this work. Not everyone writes or thinks in English. Language is not a neutral technical layer — it's the medium of thought. Impoverish the language, and you impoverish the thinking. That's not a new observation, but it's worth keeping in view when building AI systems that process, translate, or generate content at scale.

Which raises a harder question: does AI expand our cognitive reach, or does it flatten our output into statistically averaged discourse? Probably both, depending on how it's used.

## What's Next

My current focus is on **orchestrating LLMs within larger decision systems** — combining generative AI with structured business constraints rather than treating models as standalone tools. The immediate project is a decision-support agent for tender evaluation, integrating GenAI with automated document analysis and reporting workflows.
